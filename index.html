Top Tourist Destinations in Kerala

Alleppey

Famous for its beautiful backwaters and houseboat cruises.

Munnar

A hill station known for tea plantations and cool weather.

Kovalam

A popular beach with lighthouse and surfing spots.

Thekkady

Home to Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary and elephants.

Varkala

Famous for cliffs beside the Arabian Sea and holy beach.
